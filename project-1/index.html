About the Olympics

The Olympic Games are a major international multi-sport event that occurs every four years. The Games are considered the world's foremost sports competition with more than 200 nations participating.

Events

The Olympics feature a wide range of sports including athletics, swimming, gymnastics, and more. Athletes from around the world compete for gold, silver, and bronze medals in various events.
